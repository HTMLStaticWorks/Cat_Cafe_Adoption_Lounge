Our Story

Driven by Paws,
Inspired by Love.

Purr & Pour was founded on a simple belief: that every cat deserves a safe haven and every human deserves a moment of tranquility. What started as a small dream to combine specialty coffee with animal welfare has grown into the city's premier sanctuary for felines and coffee enthusiasts alike.

Our Commitment

Welfare First

The health and happiness of our resident cats are our top priority. We provide professional veterinary care and a stress-free environment.

Ethical Sourcing

From our coffee beans to our cat litter, we choose eco-friendly and ethically sourced products that respect our planet.

Community Impact

We partner with local shelters to reduce feline homelessness and educate the community on responsible pet ownership.

The Humans

Meet the Purr-fectionists

Behind every cup of coffee and every happy cat is a team of dedicated baristas, cat behaviorists, and animal lovers. We are united by our passion for hospitality and our devotion to our four-legged residents.
